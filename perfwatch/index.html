PerfWatch

PC Performance Watching

Have the Controls on Game Settings

This program is allowing to watch ingame, while playing, the performances of your PC Hardwares. With it, you can adapt your game settings, if you care in your PC performance. Does your PC needs some optimization or not? Your game or your PC can have a problem, so which one? Don't let your PC die, have the control on. Let's see...

Considerations About the Program

Press Keyboard keys F1 to put on left side the overlay, F2 to put on right side, F3 to make disapearing, and F4 to reinitialize minimum and maximum values of the FPS counter. To watch performances, your game, you are playing, must be in windowed mode. For games in windowed mode but still displaying a border at top, use keys page down and page up to remove/add the border at top, when your game is the foreground window. The program is located under bin/release folder from the code source folder in the dedicated repository. You can check here to find the right repository with same name.
