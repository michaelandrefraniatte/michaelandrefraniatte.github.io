Control Your Shoot

A Target Moving to Shoot for Reach Higher Score

This program help you to train your aim as indicating its name. Each time you clicking the shoot is earing your mind blowing on the target moving. Concentrate yourself to shoot at the target, and see your score in progress. It's like you have a medical incarry to pass through. Reach a higher score for push out your limit, if you have nothing to do to pass the time. Control your aim like a pro before go in action with your friends at night in multiplayer FPS games. Be a true gamer this time, have a bionic aim. Take control of your mind for the future. Let's see...

Considerations About the Program

The program is made if you have time lost. The sound of firing can be changed from the folder assets, where there is the default sound to replace. I made the web version of aimtraining. The program is located under bin/release folder from the code source folder in the dedicated repository. You can check here to find the right repository with same name.
